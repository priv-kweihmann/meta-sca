| SCA_LICENSECHECK_EXTRA_SUPPRESS | Extra error-IDs to be suppressed | space-separated-list | ""
| SCA_GOLICENSECHECK_MIN_CONFIDENCE | Minimum confidence about the license | float: 0.0-1.0 | "0.9"
